Education Institutions

Education institutions can log into the system or register. After logging in, they can apply for bursaries on behalf of students or check the status of previous applications, including how many students have applied for bursaries so far. Institutions can also log out to exit the system.

Admin / CDF Office

The admin or CDF office can log in to upload application details and update applicants. The office can also update content used in the application process.
